Education Ocean

Google Books

Google Books is a service from Google that searches the full text of books and magazines that Google has scanned, converted to text using optical character recognition, and stored in its digital database.

Open Accesss Texts

Open access texts are scholarly works available online free of charge. They promote the accessibility of knowledge without financial barriers. These texts span various disciplines and are valuable resources for researchers and learners worldwide.

Archieve.org

Internet Archive is a non-profit library of millions of free books, movies, software, music, websites, and more.

OverDrive

OverDrive, Inc. is a worldwide digital distributor of eBooks, audiobooks, online magazines and streaming video titles.

National Science Digital Library (NSDL)

The NSDL is an online hub with top-notch STEM resources like textbooks, multimedia, simulations, and lesson plans, catering to educators, students, and researchers. It promotes access to STEM materials and supports innovative teaching methods, invaluable for formal and informal education.
